Behind the name

Meleon is Outi

Meleon is the artist name of Outi — a Finnish psychologist, astrologer of thirty years, sound healer, yoga teacher, and visual artist working from Helsinki.

Three decades of clinical and organisational psychology taught her how minds move under pressure. Three decades of sky-reading taught her the longer weather those minds move inside. Meleonia is where the two vocabularies finally share a room — myth treated as psychology, psychology treated as myth, and sound underneath both.

The visuals are her own: painterly worlds in dusty rose and oxidised green, figures dissolving into their substrates, and a recurring field of dots that reads as nervous tissue, star chart, and cell structure at once.
